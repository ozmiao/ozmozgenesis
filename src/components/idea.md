# ⚡ OZMOZ: Digital Archaeology & Neural Revival Hub

## Problem
In the modern software era, thousands of high-potential applications and codebases are abandoned due to shifting trends or lack of resources. This "Digital Waste" represents lost architectural value and logic that could be refactored into modern high-impact products.

## Solution (The AI Role)
Ozmoz serves as a premier infrastructure where "Architects" reclaim abandoned source code. 
- **AI Core (Gemini):** Performs "Neural Audits" on legacy repositories to identify technical debt, risk areas, and suggest a modern migration strategy.
- **Revival Protocol:** AI calculates suggested bounties, deadlines, and architect capacity for each mission.

## Target User
Elite software engineers and "Digital Archaeologists" who specialize in refactoring, legacy systems, and modernizing abandoned codebases.

## Competitor Analysis
- **Generic Bounty Boards (Gitcoin, Bounties.network):** Focus on new features, not legacy revival.
- **Freelance Platforms (Upwork):** Lack the gamified "Genesis" atmosphere and technical audit tools.
- **Difference:** Ozmoz is purely dedicated to "Digital Archaeology" with an AI-driven audit layer.

## Success Criteria
Successful transition of abandoned codebases (e.g., Google Wave, Vine) into functioning modern applications validated by the Lab Lords.
